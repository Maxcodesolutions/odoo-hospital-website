Pharmacy Management

Complete pharmacy inventory and prescription management system

Overview

The Pharmacy Management module provides comprehensive control over medication inventory, prescription processing, and pharmaceutical operations. It ensures accurate stock management, prevents medication errors, and streamlines pharmacy workflows.

Key Features

Medicine Inventory & Stock Control

Real-time inventory tracking with automated stock level monitoring and low stock alerts.

Prescription Management

Digital prescription processing with drug interaction checking and dosage validation.

Automated Reorder Alerts

Smart reorder notifications based on consumption patterns and minimum stock levels.

Expiry Tracking

Comprehensive expiry date monitoring with alerts for medications nearing expiration.

Supplier & Purchase Management

Vendor management with purchase order processing and supplier performance tracking.

Benefits

  • Prevent medication stockouts and overstocking
  • Reduce medication errors through automated checks
  • Optimize inventory costs and reduce waste
  • Improve prescription accuracy and patient safety
  • Streamline pharmacy operations and workflows
