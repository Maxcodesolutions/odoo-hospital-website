Do Not Sell My Personal Information

Exercise your privacy rights and control over your personal data

Your Privacy Rights

At Maxcode Solutions, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Under various privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other applicable regulations, you have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information.

What This Means

The "sale" of personal information refers to the disclosure of your personal information to a third party for monetary or other valuable consideration. This includes:

  • Sharing your information with advertising networks
  • Providing your data to analytics companies
  • Disclosing information to data brokers
  • Sharing data with social media platforms for advertising purposes

Our Commitment

Maxcode Solutions does not sell your personal information in the traditional sense. However, we want to be transparent about how your information may be used and give you control over your data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate our website and provide services
  • Analytics Partners: To understand how our website is used and improve our services
  • Marketing Partners: Only with your explicit consent

How to Opt-Out

You can exercise your right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information in several ways:

1. Online Form

Complete our opt-out request form below to submit your request electronically.

2. Email Request

Send an email to privacy@maxcodesolutions.in with the subject line "Do Not Sell My Personal Information"

3. Phone Request

Call us at +91 9560455637 and ask to speak with our privacy team.

Opt-Out Request Form

What Happens After You Submit

Once we receive your opt-out request:

  • Verification: We will verify your identity to protect your privacy
  • Processing: Your request will be processed within 45 days (or 90 days if extended)
  • Confirmation: You will receive confirmation of your request
  • Implementation: We will stop selling your personal information

Other Privacy Rights

In addition to the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information, you may also have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Correct: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
