Complete Hospital Management System Implementation

Delhi, India 2024 6 Months Implementation
500+ Beds

Project Overview

A leading multi-specialty hospital in Delhi approached Maxcode Solutions to implement a comprehensive Hospital Management System to streamline their operations across 500+ beds, 50+ departments, and 200+ doctors. The hospital was facing challenges with manual processes, long patient wait times, and inefficient resource management.

Challenges Faced

  • Manual patient registration and appointment scheduling
  • Disconnected systems across departments
  • Long patient wait times and queue management issues
  • Inefficient inventory and pharmacy management
  • Complex billing and insurance processing
  • Limited visibility into hospital operations

Solution Implemented

We implemented a comprehensive Hospital Management System built on Odoo ERP, customized specifically for the healthcare industry. The solution integrated all hospital operations into a unified platform.

Key Modules Implemented

Patient Management System

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) with complete patient history
  • Digital patient registration and profile management
  • Appointment scheduling with automated reminders
  • Queue management system for OPD and IPD
  • Patient portal for online appointments and reports

Clinical Management

  • Doctor and specialist management
  • Treatment planning and progress tracking
  • Prescription management with drug interaction alerts
  • Clinical decision support system
  • Medical history and allergy tracking

Pharmacy & Inventory Management

  • Automated inventory tracking and reorder management
  • Drug stock management with expiry tracking
  • Pharmacy billing and prescription dispensing
  • Supplier management and procurement
  • Cost analysis and budget control

Laboratory & Radiology Integration

  • Test requisition and result management
  • Radiology image storage and retrieval
  • Automated report generation
  • Quality control and compliance tracking
  • Integration with diagnostic equipment

Billing & Insurance

  • Automated billing for all services
  • Insurance claim processing and tracking
  • Payment gateway integration
  • Financial reporting and analytics
  • Tax compliance and audit trails

Implementation Process

Phase 1: Discovery & Planning (2 weeks)

Phase 2: Development & Customization (3 months)

  • Continuous improvement and feedback
